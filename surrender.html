If you must surrender your dog, please fill out the surrender form below. Please note that because 2DaRescue is entirely foster-based, our space is extremely limited. Filling out the form in no way guarantees that we shall be able to take your pet.

Surrender Form

By completing this form, I, acknowledge that I am the owner of the dog described below.

I am requesting 2DaRescue to take possession of my dog(s). I willfully surrender all ownership rights to the rescue organization. I understand that I will receive no compensation for said dog(s) nor will I have the opportunity to reclaim them or receive future information regarding them unless specifically noted by the organization.

Neither 2DaRescue, its volunteers or associates will be held liable or responsible for any claims or causes of actions incurred as a result of the provision or restriction of information, services, contracts, adoptions, fostering or veterinary care, or by the actions of any and all dogs transferred or referenced herein.

2DaRescue agrees not to sell my information. Furthermore, 2DaRescue will work in the best interest of the dog to find him/her a suitable home. If a suitable home cannot be found, 2DaRescue will contact me using the contact information provided below to give me the opportunity to reclaim my dog. If I do not exercise this right within forty eight (48) hours, 2DaRescue will take whatever action deemed appropriate.
